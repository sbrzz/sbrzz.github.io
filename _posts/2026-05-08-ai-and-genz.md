On Thursday, May 7, 2026, I had the pleasure of delivering a training lecture on AI at [CreTa Innovation Hub][creta], in collaboration with [Cantiere AI][cantiere], represented by Fabio Dal Maso and Davide Bonvicini, a company specialized in AI training programs for both the public and private sectors.

The lecture, titled “AI for Gen Z: Ally or Replacement?”, focused on the impact of this technology on younger generations. We discussed both the opportunities AI offers for learning and growth, as well as potential risks such as cognitive debt and loneliness.

The audience consisted mainly of high school teachers and parents interested in understanding the technology and helping younger generations make the most of it.
